Evita

$1,230.00

Evita is a wedding dress that beautifully combines the mermaid silhouette with gentle tulle draping and a touch of glamorous shine. The main fabric used here is soft Chantilly lace adorned with tiny clear sequins that give off a subtle sparkle.

The dress comes in a light ivory color, a classic shade that suits various skin tones. The corset top is further enhanced with tulle draping and elegant lace appliques, adding texture and interest to the overall design.

One of the best features of the Evita dress is its versatility. It comes with detachable sleeves that you can style in two different ways. You can wear them as full on-shoulder sleeves for a more classic look, or go off-shoulder for a more relaxed and trendy feel.
